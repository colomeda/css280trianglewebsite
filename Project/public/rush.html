Triangle Rush

Men's Greek life rush at Rose-Hulman starts on Monday of the second week of the academic fall quarter and lasts for six weeks. During this time, freshman can learn about all the fraternities including us.

Our rush calendar is in the calendar section of our website. If you can, come out to the events during rush so we can get to know you and so you can get to know us.
